Chili Con Carne

Simple and easy.

Another very simple meal to make, a chili con carne will fill you up with next to no effort at all. Using very few ingredients the only vegetable to prepare is a single onion. Honestly, all the flavour comes from the red kidney beans in chilli sauce. One caveat when it comes to the rice, please use a rice cooker. the rice comes out perfect every time.

Ingredients

  • 500g minced beef
  • 2 tins red kidney beans in chilli sauce
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1 white onion
  • beef stock
  • lazy chilli (optional)
  • 1/2 cup of rice

Instructions

  1. Peel and dice the onion
  2. fry the onion in the frying pan with oil of choice
  3. add lazy chilli for extra heat
  4. add mince and beef stock and fry until evenly brown
  5. add the tins of red kidney beans and the tine of chopped tomatoes
  6. leave to cook for a while or until some of the liquid has evaporated
  7. serve when ready with a fluffy bed of rice or mix it in with the chilli con carne

Nice, simple and delicious, takes no time at all. You could even add a potato to boil in with the chilli to soak up all the flavour.
