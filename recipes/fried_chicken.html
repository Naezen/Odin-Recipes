Fried Chicken in flour and egg

Messy but delicious

Another favourite of mine, it can be messy to prepare but so satisfying when complete. I use Polish chicken seasoning to give the chicken an incredible flavour but you can use whatever spices you prefer. Served with steamed broccoli and boiled potatoes, garnished with dill, and butter, this dish feels clean and filling.

Ingredients:

  • Mini chicken fillets
  • 2 eggs
  • plain flour
  • chicken seasoning
  • red potatoes
  • broccoli
  • dill

Instructions:

  1. First prepare bowls for egg, flour and spices
  2. peel and quater potatoes to boil in a saucepan
  3. cut broccoli and add to a steamer with some salt
  4. add oil to frying pan on high heat
  5. test the heat of oil with a drop of egg, it should fry immediately
  6. dip the chicken fillets in egg, flour and spices then add to the pan
  7. fry each piece for no longer than a couple of minutes or until cooked through
  8. serve chicken onto plate with broccoli and potatoes, adding dill and butter

A fantastic, simple meal, full of flavour to enjoy anytime of the year.
