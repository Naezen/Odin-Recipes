Polish Cauliflower Soup

More sour, less cream.

Cauliflower soup is delicious, warming and incredibly easy to make. All it takes to make are a few vegetables, (including of course cauliflower), thrown into a pot of water with usually chicken stock, dill, black pepper and that's it, just leave it to boil for a couple of hours and it's done. Serve with a teaspoon of sour cream which gives it its defining eauropean flavour.

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes,
  • Carrots,
  • Leek,
  • Cauliflower,
  • Celery,
  • Stock (chicken or vegetable),
  • Dill,
  • Black pepper
